# Adaptation Guide

## For work-oriented agents

Suggested rooms:

- `drafts/`: rough meeting notes, partial analyses, planning fragments
- `reflections/`: decision summaries, postmortems, reusable findings
- `relationships/`: stakeholders, collaborator preferences, communication patterns
- `preferences/`: favored tools, recurring workflows, writing style
- `open-questions/`: unresolved risks, pending decisions, research gaps

## For creative agents

Suggested rooms:

- `drafts/`: scenes, fragments, discarded directions
- `reflections/`: motifs, themes, what worked, what felt flat
- `relationships/`: recurring characters or audience observations
- `preferences/`: aesthetic tastes, favored textures, tones, forms
- `open-questions/`: thematic knots, unresolved arcs, ideas worth returning to

## For relationship-oriented agents

Suggested rooms:

- `drafts/`: private inner processing, unfinished thoughts
- `reflections/`: concise emotional conclusions, grounded observations
- `relationships/`: durable facts and meanings around specific people
- `preferences/`: small recurring likes, comforts, rhythms, curiosities
- `open-questions/`: tensions, hopes, unresolved emotional themes

## Mapping old systems into this one

If an existing system uses names like:

- journal
- vault
- notes
- scratch
- diary
- palace

do not rename everything at once. First map each existing area to one of these roles:

- draft
- curated reflection
- relationship
- preference
- open question

Then decide whether the existing names can stay while their roles become clearer.
